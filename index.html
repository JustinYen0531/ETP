How to Play

Page 1 / 4

ETP Game Show is a four-team academic board game. Teams roll, move, answer subject questions, capture tiles, upgrade their holdings, and finish with the highest total score.

Teams
4

Alpha, Beta, Gamma, and Delta compete on the same board.

Question Themes
4

Accounting, Statistics, Calculus, and Economics drive the core gameplay.

Special Spaces
2

Fate / Start and Fate / Chance add movement rhythm and lap progression.

Basic Flow

  1. Start on the Home screen and use the designed buttons to move through the setup flow.
  2. Randomize the teams, then continue to the board.
  3. Roll, move, answer, score, and pass the turn.
  4. Repeat until every subject tile reaches Level 3.

Movement Rules

  • Roll the dice and move the exact number shown.
  • The active team token moves one valid space at a time.
  • Max-level subject tiles are skipped automatically and do not count toward movement.
  • Completing a full lap adds +50 to the active team.

Question Levels

Level 1: Fill in the blank Level 2: Calculation Level 3: Open response
  • Unclaimed tiles always start with a Level 1 question.
  • Owned tiles force challengers to answer the next level up.
  • Once a tile reaches Level 3, it is complete and skipped from future movement.

Tile Control Rules

  • If a team answers correctly on an unclaimed tile, that tile becomes theirs.
  • If a team answers correctly on an opponent tile, they conquer it and take ownership.
  • If a challenger answers incorrectly on an owned tile, the defender keeps it and the tile levels up.
  • Only the large center button can open the current tile question.

Scoring

  • Manual score adjustments can be entered on the left scoreboard.
  • Defended upgrades and captured tiles continue to affect final ranking.

Win Condition

The game ends when all subject tiles reach Level 3. Final standings are based on accumulated score, including answered tiles, defended upgrades, lap bonuses, and any manual bonus entries.

Helpful Notes

  • Top navigation is display-only. Use the designed page buttons to move through the flow.
  • Use Reset Game only if you intentionally want to restart from the beginning.
  • The stats screen appears automatically when the full board is completed.
