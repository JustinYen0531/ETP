## 1. 學科題庫 (Academic Question Bank)

### 📊 會計 (Accounting)

#### ACQ1 - 出題者：Kelly (Tile 1)
*   **Level 1 (填空):** Accounts that represent what the business owes to others are called ______.
    *   **答案:** Liabilities
*   **Level 2 (計算):** If a company buys a $5,000 equipment with Cash, what is the net effect on Total Assets? (Increase/Decrease/No Change)
    *   **答案:** No Change
*   **Level 3 (開放):** Why is the Income Statement prepared for a 'period of time' instead of a 'single point in time'?
    *   **答案:** Because it records the financial performance (flow) over a duration, unlike the Balance Sheet which is a stock concept for a single point in time.

#### ACQ2 - 出題者：Carrie (Tile 5)
*   **Level 1 (填空):** The resources owned by a business that are expected to provide future economic benefits are called ______.
    *   **答案:** Assets
*   **Level 2 (計算):** If a company has Total Assets of $50,000 and Total Equity of $20,000, what is the total amount of its Liabilities? (Enter number only)
    *   **答案:** 30000
*   **Level 3 (開放):** Explain the concept of 'Depreciation' and why it is recorded as an expense even if no cash flow occurs during the period.
    *   **答案:** Depreciation represents the systematic allocation of an asset's cost over its useful life to match expenses with revenue earned (Matching Principle).

### 📈 統計 (Statistics)

#### STQ1 - 出題者：Ally (Tile 2)
*   **Level 1 (填空):** According to the Central Limit Theorem, as the sample size becomes sufficiently large, the sampling distribution of the sample mean approaches a ______ distribution, regardless of the population's original shape.
    *   **答案:** Normal
*   **Level 2 (計算):** If a dataset has a population mean of μ=75 and a standard deviation of σ=5, what is the Z-score for a data point with a value of X=85?
    *   **答案:** 2
*   **Level 3 (開放):** When looking at housing prices in a city, or starting salaries for college graduates, why is the 'Median' often a better measure of a typical value than the 'Mean'?
    *   **答案:** The mean is highly sensitive to extreme values (outliers), which can distort the average.

#### STQ2 - 出題者：Justin (Tile 6)
*   **Level 1 (填空):** A probability distribution where the mean, median, and mode are all equal and the curve is perfectly bell-shaped is the ______ distribution.
    *   **答案:** Normal
*   **Level 2 (計算):** In a standard deck of 52 cards, what is the probability of drawing a 'Heart' card? (Enter as a fraction, e.g., 1/2)
    *   **答案:** 1/4
*   **Level 3 (開放):** Explain the main difference between 'Descriptive Statistics' and 'Inferential Statistics'.
    *   **答案:** Descriptive statistics summarize and describe data, while inferential statistics use sample data to make predictions or generalizations about a larger population.

### 📐 微積分 (Calculus)

#### CAQ1 - 出題者：Ally (Tile 3)
*   **Level 1 (填空):** The Fundamental Theorem of Calculus establishes a connection between the two main operations of calculus: differentiation and ______.
    *   **答案:** Integration
*   **Level 2 (計算):** Evaluate the definite integral: ∫²₀ 3x^2 dx
    *   **答案:** 8
*   **Level 3 (開放):** If a function s(t) represents the position of a car at time t, what do its first derivative s′(t) and second derivative s′′(t) represent in the real world?
    *   **答案:** The first derivative s′(t) represents the car's Velocity, and the second derivative s′′(t) represents its Acceleration.

#### CAQ2 - 出題者：Justin (Tile 7)
*   **Level 1 (填空):** The mathematical concept used to measure the rate at which a function changes at a specific point is the ______.
    *   **答案:** Derivative
*   **Level 2 (計算):** Find the derivative (f') of f(x) = 5x^2 + 7x - 3.
    *   **答案:** 10x + 7
*   **Level 3 (開放):** In calculus, what does it mean for a function to be 'Continuous' at a specific point?
    *   **答案:** It means the limit as you approach the point equals the actual function value at that point (there are no jumps, holes, or gaps in the graph).

### 💰 經濟 (Economics)

#### ECQ1 - 出題者：Kelly (Tile 4)
*   **Level 1 (填空):** A situation where the quantity supplied exceeds the quantity demanded at the current price is called a ______.
    *   **答案:** Surplus
*   **Level 2 (計算):** If the price of a cup of coffee increases by 20% and the quantity demanded falls by 10%, what is the Price Elasticity of Demand? (Enter number only)
    *   **答案:** 0.5
*   **Level 3 (開放):** Explain why a 'Price Ceiling' (such as rent control) often leads to a shortage in the long run.
    *   **答案:** Because the price is fixed below the equilibrium, quantity demanded increases while quantity supplied decreases, creating a gap (shortage).

#### ECQ2 - 出題者：Carrie (Tile 8)
*   **Level 1 (填空):** When the price of a good increases, the Quantity Demanded usually decreases; this relationship is known as the Law of ______.
    *   **答案:** Demand
*   **Level 2 (計算):** If a consumer has a budget of $120 and the price of a book is $15, how many books can they purchase at most?
    *   **答案:** 8
*   **Level 3 (開放):** Explain why a consumer's willingness to pay for a product often decreases as they consume more units of that same product.
    *   **答案:** Due to the Law of Diminishing Marginal Utility, the satisfaction (utility) gained from each additional unit decreases, reducing the willingness to pay.
